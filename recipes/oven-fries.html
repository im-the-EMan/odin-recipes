Oven-baked Fries

Photo by Michael Kraus

Description

This recipe will have us make delicious fries to go with our pizza. Instead of using a deepfryer, however, these will be made in our oven. Recipe by Melissa Clark.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of russet potatoes, cut into 1/4 inch sticks
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F
  2. In a bowel, toss the cut potatoes, oil, salt and pepper together until evenly coated. Spread the poataoes out on a baking sheet and roast for 15 minutes or until golden and crispy.
  3. Reduce heat to 425°F and bake for an additional 10 to 15 minutes.
