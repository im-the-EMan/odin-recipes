Pizza Recipe

Photo from Inspired Taste

Description

Pizza Sauce

  • 1 (15-ounce) can Muir Glen Organic Tomato Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, grated on a Microplane
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Classic Cheese Pizza

  • 1 ball Artisan Pizza Dough
  • All-purpose flour, for dusting
  • Semolina flour, for dusting
  • 1/4 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 3 ounces shredded mozzarella
  • Fresh Oregano leaves

Steps

  1. Stir in all Sauce ingredients in a bowl, put to the side.
  2. Let dough stand at room temprature until cool, 1 to 2 hours. Transfer ball to floured surface and firmly press fingertips al over dough, leaving a 1/2 inch border.
  3. Preheat oven to 500°F, lay dough on Semolina-dusted pizza peel and top with our pizza sauce and mozzarella.
  4. Using the pizza peel, load onto a pan and bake until pizza is cooked through, around 9 minutes.
  5. Transfer to cutting board. Garnish with oregano and cut into slices. Enjoy!
