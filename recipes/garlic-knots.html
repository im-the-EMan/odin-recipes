Garlic Knots

Description

This recipe will have us make another popular side for our pizza, Garlic Knots! This easy recipe also goes well with our other side Oven-baked Fries.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Sugar
  • Warm water
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt

Garlic Topping

  • Grated Garlic
  • Melted Butter

Steps

  1. Make the dough by combining the flour, sugar, warm water, yeast and salt in a stand mixer. Then turn it on a lightly floured surface and knead a few times. Form the dough into a ball and let it rest in a bowl brushed with olive oil and covered in plastic wrap until double in size.
  2. After it has risen properly, knead it more and turn it on a floured surface, then cut into 8 pieces of equal size. Roll each piece into a 9-inch rope and tie it into the knot shape.
  3. Place the knots on a baking sheet with parchment paper and bake at 450°F for 11 to 13 minutes in the oven. Brush with the garlic topping.
